Construction Skills People

The UK's leading provider of construction-related training

Established in 2008, Construction Skills People deliver a wide range of vocational qualifications and commercial training courses specifically for the construction sector. Our in-depth industry knowledge allows us to advise our clients through the complex minefield of Health & Safety legislation and regulation.

For principal contractors working under strict section 106 requirements, we are able to offer solutions that deliver results, thanks to our ability to deliver Apprenticeships, Traineeships and Pre-employment courses that help you deliver your required outcomes. Our first priority is you and we train our staff to ensure we understand your needs and requirements before proposing any training solution.
